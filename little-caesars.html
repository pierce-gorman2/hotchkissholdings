Little Caesars

The brand that launched the Hotchkiss family’s restaurant journey in San Antonio and helped establish the foundation for the company’s long-term growth.

Foundational brand, long-term legacy.

Little Caesars is where the Hotchkiss story began. When Lee and Kathy Hotchkiss brought the first Little Caesars to San Antonio in 1984, they began building the operating experience, market knowledge, and family business culture that still drive Hotchkiss Holdings today.
